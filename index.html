About Me

Biography: Aidan Waterman

Aidan Waterman is a sophomore at Howard County Community College. He is majoring in Audio Visual Media Production and Social Sciences and is considering a career in either community service or filmmaking. His interest in filmmaking started with an interest in making videos as a young child. Through courses (Principles of Film and Media Production, Concept and ‘Story Development, and Introduction to Electronic Media), a summer VFX workshop with Exceptional Minds, two summer jobs working on promotional videos for clients, and my own research and personal projects, Aidan learned a great deal about filmmaking, visual storytelling and special effects. However, he still has a lot to learn and he is eager to learn it.

His interest in community service was started by a personal moral commitment to reducing the amount of evil in the world. Relating to this, he also won a community service award in high school, and volunteers at the North East Social Action Program. He’s planning on going into either community service or filmmaking and aspires to be a strong, driven moral leader who builds people up.
